Multinomial logit model

In the experiment, people had three choices after being shown a text: attacking the exogroup, defend the endogroup or do nothing (\(k\) choices, in our case, \(k\) = 3). Here we will set choice 1 (attacking exogroup) as the reference category #(checar que 1 é atacar o exogrupo). The model fitted is a multinomial Bayesian regression.

Multinomial logit model

The data is comprised of three independent studies.
